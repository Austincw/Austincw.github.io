Character of the Week




Palutena one of my personal favorites as well as the goto pick for many other smash players in a matchup against harass-type players. She has good range on her basic side-smashes and the largest up-smash hitbox.

Abilities:
  • Neutral B: Throws a volley of 3 projectiles from ascending heights which fly directly to the current spot of an automatically selected target. This hits inconsistently but can be advantageous for staggering opponents as you fall to the ground.
  • Up B: Recovery similar to that of Pitt and Rosaline & Luma.
  • Side B: This can bounce all projectile attacks coming at you from one direction to where they came from, it covers a wide area from the bottom of Palutena to above her head
  • Down B: Does not have marginally more or less frames than other standard counters
Although some would argue that her speed is moderate and her damage leaves something desired. Her combat potential is in-part derived from her moveset and synergizes well with sub-par harass. With a cautious mindset and a reactive playstyle, she can shine in almost any matchup as an all-around good player.
